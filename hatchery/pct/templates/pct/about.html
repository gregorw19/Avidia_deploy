About The Hatchery

The Hatchery is committed to providing a space where all students, faculty, and staff members of Boston College can feel welcome. Below are some of our guiding principles, as well as some of our major milestones on this journey.

Mission Statement

In the spirit of Think to Make for Impact, being able to iterate, visualize, refine, and communicate creative design ideas using prototypes is an essential skill set for our students entering the future workforce. The mission of the Boston College (BC) Makerspaces (The Hatchery) is to enable these transformative capabilities for everyone—regardless of background or discipline—by providing an equitable, accessible, and well-equipped community-oriented space. Wherever you are on your making and design journey, The Hatchery encourages all members of the community to let their ideas fly.

On this site, you will be able to:

  • Book time on different Hatchery equipment
  • Sign up for training sessions and get machine certifications
  • Manage your profile and view your certifications
  • Reserve the makerspace for events and projects
