FAQs

What is The Hatchery?

The Hatchery is Boston College’s first community makerspace, located on the 2nd and 3rd floors of 245 Beacon St.

Who can use The Hatchery?

The Hatchery Makerspaces are open to all Boston College students, faculty, and staff, regardless of school or department.

How can I access The Hatchery?

To join the maker community at The Hatchery, take our brief online Makerspace Orientation. Once complete, you’ll gain access to QReserve where you can book Tool Trainings and equipment.

I am a faculty member. Can I use The Hatchery for my academic course?

Yes! Visit our Collaboration Page to learn how your course can partner with The Hatchery.

Can my on-campus club/organization hold an event with The Hatchery?

The Hatchery partners with clubs, teams, organizations, and departments. Visit our Collaboration Page for more details.

I still have more questions. Where can I get the answers?

Visit our Contact page or stop by to speak with a Hatchery Team Member.
